4D Monster Lobsters

Formed in 2019, 4D Monster Lobsters quickly managed to find starting point at local music scene by developing an effective, driven and psychedelic sound. The band released debut EP titled Secret Missions, which was followed by a full length album Solitary Days of Alien Abduction, which is already available on all major streaming platforms. Currently the band is working on developing new sound including much more dynamic, emotional and even ethnic vibe to it, which can already be outlined in the newly released singles
