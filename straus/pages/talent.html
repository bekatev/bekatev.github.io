We Buy Talent

Straus Entertainment works as a mediator between the promoter and the artist, connects local and international festivals, companies and municipalities with the artists and bands from all kinds of styles & genres as a booking agency.

Our Experience

In past 10 years, we have booked and promoted over 800 acts on different stages, gigs and festivals. Among them Placebo, Deep Purple, Franz Ferdinand, Tom Odell, Alt J, Air, Mogwai, Roisin Murphy, Zemfira, Beth Hart, etc. Shows booked by us had an attendance of over a million in total.

We also concentrate on electronic music scene, with focus on techno and house, have previously worked with David August, Kollektiv Turmstrasse, Stephan Bodzin, Gui Boratto, Moodymann, Infected Mushrooms and other world's top electronic artists
